CROSSFIT?


CrossFit is constantly varied functional movements performed at relatively high intensity. All CrossFit
workouts are based on functional movements, and these movements reflect the best aspects of gymnastics,
weightlifting, running, rowing and more. These are the core movements of life. They move the largest loads
the longest distances, so they are ideal for maximizing the amount of work done in the shortest time.
Intensity is essential for results and is measurable as work divided by time or power. The more work you do
in less time, or the higher the power output, the more intense the effort.
